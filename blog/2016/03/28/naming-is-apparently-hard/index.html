Naming Is Apparently Hard

Mar 28th, 2016 8:12 pm

Naming Variables

There are certain properties that variable names should always communicate. By reading the name of the variable, the reader should be able to answer the three following questions:

  1. Is it 1) a singular thing or 2) a plural thing?
  2. Is it 1) a thing with attributes, or is it 2) an attribute of a thing, or is it 3) just a lone primitive?
  3. Is it a boolean?
